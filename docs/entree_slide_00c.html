Introduction to ALife

ALife/ADiscipline

  • An open-ended project of exploring life-as-it-could-be, beyond life-as-we-know-it (LAICB >> LAWKI)
  • ...more of a viewpoint or philosophy of inquiry than a discipline per se.
  • ...makes heavy use of 'lateral idea transfer' between different fields, borrowing tools and perspectives from physics, chemistry, computation, mathematics, complexity......
  • 'Artificial Life' encompasses the art and science of exploring real and abstract living processes.
