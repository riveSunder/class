ALife Influences in the Real World


"BEAM" robots by Simon Fraser. These are simple, solar-powered analog robots, typically with only one or two "neurons" built from a couple of transistors and a voltage sensitive integrated circuit (or diode). These simple circuits create goal-directed (phototropic) behavior, seeking the light that powers their circuits. See also: Braitenberg vehicle.
