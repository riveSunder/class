What is Life?


  chemistry open to selection...?
      ...program for symbolic self-replication?
          animate matter defying thermodynamic equilibrium?

  • "Life is a self-sustaining chemical system capable of Darwinian evolution" NASA working definition, referenced by and often attributed to Gerald Joyce 1994
  • "A a self-organized non-equilibrium system such that B its processes are governed by a program which is stored symbolically and C it can reproduce itself, including the program." Lee Smolin. 'Life of the Cosmos'. 1997. p. 156
  • "[...] it goes on 'doing something', moving, exchanging material with its environment, and so forth, and that for a much longer period than we would expect of an inanimate piece of matter to 'keep going' under similar circumstances." Schrödinger, 1944.
